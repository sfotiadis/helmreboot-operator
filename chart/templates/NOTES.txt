HelmReboot Operator has been deployed successfully!

The operator will now monitor your Flux HelmRelease resources and automatically
restart them when they fail due to timeout errors.

To check the operator status:
  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "helmreboot-operator.name" . }}

To view operator logs:
  kubectl logs -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "helmreboot-operator.name" . }}

To check metrics:
  kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc/{{ include "helmreboot-operator.fullname" . }} 8080:8080

For more information, visit: https://github.com/sfotiadis/helmreboot-operator
